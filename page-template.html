What/Why?

Koala is a mobile app that allows users to compose high quality songs on their mobile device using sounds they’ve recorded through their microphoe, or sounds they’ve downloaded onto their device.

Begginers often don’t have access to large sound libraries when they begin, and while searching for samples is fun for some it is a chore for many.

KSamples is a community for sharing, disovering, and organiziang samples on your phone. User may upload and download sounds, curate sound packs, retrieve KSample sounds from the Koala app, and show love by liking other users samples.
